SOP: Fever Management in OPD
Department: General Medicine
Severity Indicators:
- Temp > 103F: High Risk
- Accompanied by headache and stiff neck: Meningitis Protocol
- Lasting > 5 days: Malaria/Dengue Screening required
Diagnostic Steps:
1. Check SpO2
2. CBC (Complete Blood Count)
3. MP/Widal if persistent
Special Instructions:
- Advise hydration.
- Tepid sponging.
