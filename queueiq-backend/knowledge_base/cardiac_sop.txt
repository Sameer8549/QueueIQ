SOP: Acute Chest Pain
Department: Cardiology / Emergency
Protocol:
- Immediate ECG within 10 minutes.
- Check Troponin-T levels.
- Pulse oxymetry and supplemental oxygen if SpO2 < 94%.
Red Flags:
- Pain radiating to left arm/jaw.
- Profuse sweating (Diaphoresis).
- Nausea/Shortness of breath.
Triage:
- Level 1: Immediate Resuscitation for suspected MI.
- Level 2: Emergent if stable but high clinical suspicion.
